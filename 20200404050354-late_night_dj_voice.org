** Use a low, soft voice to open the negotiation
*** Reduces tension
*** Non-threatening
*** Slows emotional response
** 10 - 20% of the time
*** Use sparingly
** Do not use the assertive voice
*** "Always counterproductive"
*** "feels like a punch in the noise"
